--- section: featured_content sub_title: Feature panels lead: Feature panels act as a form of navigation, allowing you to promote content in an interactive and visual way. nav_order: 1 ---


They are predominantly for use on homepages or key landing pages. On rare occasions, they may add interest when used sparingly on a content page.

They do not provide detail, but act as a top-level overview, giving a taste of a theme or aspect they are promoting, providing links to further details. Text character length needs to be restricted in order to present well across various screen sizes.

Successful feature panels depend on sourcing suitable media, video and images, which need to be in certain aspcts ratios with consideration to where subject is focussed and grouped according according to our principles in creating a vertical rhythm.
