Keep content length short and equal

These feature are for giving a short teaser of the content that it links to. Keep them brief and focussed.

Keep the design looking neat and balanced by using equal amounts of content for each feature. If headings and description text vary, the visual design will look unbalanced and messy. Follow the guidelines below for suggested character limits.

Image

You need to prepare an image to the aspect ratio of 3:2 (1024x683px).

Heading

Keep headings as short and focussed as possible. On a desktop, try avoid going on to three lines, but 1-2 lines is best, (about 55 characters).

Description

On a desktop 2-3 lines is best (about 85 characters), 4 lines maximum.
